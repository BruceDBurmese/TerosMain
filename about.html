What is the LUX MUNDIS CUP 2024?

The LUX MUNDIS CUP 2024 is an extraordinary event that brought together talented individuals from across the globe to compete in various thrilling challenges. From intense sports tournaments to breathtaking performances, it was an event of a lifetime.

Participating in this event was an honor and an unforgettable experience. I learned so much and met incredible people who shared a passion for excellence.

My Experience During the Event

Being part of the LUX MUNDIS CUP 2024 especially being a candidate for the MR. and MS. TCGC 2024 for my institute; THE ICS, was a whirlwind adventure. Everyday was exhausting, practicing from daytime to sun down, midnight to be specific. It was not easy to be a candidate as there are so much pressure. When the night came, Sports Competition, Me and Erika practiced Taekwondo, because we decided to choose taekwondo as I am a taekwondo blackbelt already. So it was no hard for me to perform, the only thing is I am teaching her and it went out cool. We did not entered the top 3 but it was fine, it was our first time anyways.

The Coronation night came, we were all so nervous. From the production number to the attire competitions, I did my best for my institute. I didnt include the Top 3, but I learned so much and It doesnt matter to me. The important thing is that I've learned things.
